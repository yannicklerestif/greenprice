How Was The Ecological Price For Paris to New York Round Trip Computed?

Paris to New York Round Trip is a CO2 Object.

The ecological price of CO2 objects, by definition, only depends on the CO2 emitted to create them.

The ecological footprint, in global square meters, of a CO2 object, is computed by multiplying its CO2 cost by the ecological footprint of CO2 emissions.

To know more about what an ecological footprint or a global square meter is, see the global details page.

The ecological footprint of CO2 emissions is computed by the Global Footprint Network:

CO2 ecological footprint: 2.56 gm² / kg

The CO2 emissions for Paris to New York Round Trip are:

CO2 emissions for Paris to New York Round Trip: 1,300.00 kg

We can then compute the ecological footprint:

Ecological footprint for Paris to New York Round Trip: 2.56 x 1,300.00 = 3,328.00 gm²

We can then compute the ecological price by multiplying the ecological price by the price of a global square meter (€0.673) computed in the global details page

Ecological price for Paris to New York round trip: 3,328.00 x 0.673 = €2,240.86
